Vjay

Actor| Singer| Politician

His full name is Joseph Vijay. He was born on June 22, 1974, at Madras, to veteran Tamil film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and Shoba Chandrasekhar who is a Carnatic vocalist. He had a sister named Vidhya who died at the age of 2 due to ill health. After her death, Vijay who was once naughty and talkative became very silent.
